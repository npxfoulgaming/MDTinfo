Scrapping crowbars is a good way, yet a tedious way to turn knives that you don't need into scrap metal that can later be sold either at the warehouse or to mechanics.

1. Make sure you have a Crowbar on you

2. Head over to the processing crate

**The crate can be found in Paleto Bay in a lot across from the Hospital

What you get from it:

- 1x Crowbar turns into 1x Scrap Metal
