Making meth is a good way to show your skills inside of an RV and also make pretty good income.

1. Make sure you have the following things:

5x Acetone - ~$75 per

5 Lithium Batteries - ~$150 per

Meth Table (Find in Robbing Trailers) - ~$1k per

“Breaking Bad” RV Camper - Steal it

2. Head on over to any tunnel up north and get into the back seat of the RV

Picture of the RV:

Then you have to answer some questions. The more you get right, the more meth you get at the end:

You get around 15 - 25 bags per run

Where can it be sold?
