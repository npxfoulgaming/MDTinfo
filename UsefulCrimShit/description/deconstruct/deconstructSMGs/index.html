Deconstructing SMGs is a good way of getting rid of an old or broken weapon without throwing it away and also getting some materials back that could be sold

1. Make sure you have SMGs to scrap

2. Head over to the deconstructing table

**The table can be found off of East Joshua Road in Sandy Shores behind an abandoned gas station
