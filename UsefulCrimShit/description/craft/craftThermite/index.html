Crafting thermite is an easy way to gain materials in order to do what you love doing, robbing banks. All you really need is steel and aluminium.

1. Make sure you have Aluminium and Steel on you

1.1 (Things you need):

3 Steel + 2 Aluminum = 1 Thermite

6 Steel + 4 Aluminum = 2 Thermite

9 Steel + 6 Aluminum = 3 Thermite

12 Steel + 8 Aluminum = 4 Thermite

2. Head over to the processing crate where you can make the powder needed

**The crafting can be found down at the Merryweather docks.
