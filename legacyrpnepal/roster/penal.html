PC. 1101 Accessory Before The Fact (ACC-BTF) Any person not present when the crime itself is committed, but has knowledge of the crime before or after the fact, and may assist in its commission. 25% Seize
PC. 1102 Accessory After The Fact (ACC-ATF) Any person who aids or abets someone who has committed a crime after the crime has been committed with the intent to help the person avoid arrest or punishment. 75% Seize
PC. 1103 Aiding and Abetting (AAA) Any person who aids in the active commission of a crime shall be given the same punishment. 100% Seize
