Definitions
Code Word Definition
P.C.101Public ServantAny person employed by the EMS, BCFD, State, or the DOJ, including Bar Certified individuals, the Vice Governor, Governor, Judges and Chief Justice.
P.C.102Peace OfficerAny Police Officer, State Trooper, Highway Patrol, Sheriff Deputy, County Sheriff, or Department of Corrections Officer.
P.C.103Notary PublicAny person employed directly by the DOJ. This person can notarise affidavits.
P.C.104Reasonable SuspicionA specific, justifiable suspicion that is based on specific facts or circumstances about a specific individual.
P.C.105Probable Cause (PC)Reasonable grounds for believing a person is guilty of a criminal act, allowing the search, arrest, prosecution, or trial of such a person.
P.C.106PerAny charge labelled with Per should be given once for each contraband seized.
P.C.107SeizeContraband, weapons, money, substances, and/or vehicles must be seized indefinitely if related to the crime. Do not return unless a Judge specifies otherwise.
P.C.108PointsPenalty points are issued for vehicular offences. They stay on a person's driving record and they lose their licence if they collect more than 15.
P.C.109PossessionContraband (or replicas) having been or is on their person, in their vehicle, or in their estate.
P.C.109Class A SubstanceCocaine, Methamphetamine (Meth), or Psilocybe Cyanescens (Shrooms), including synthetic alternative
P.C.111Class B SubstanceMarijuana (Weed), Lysergic Acid Diethylamide (Acid), or Lean, including synthetic analogue.
P.C.112Class 1 WeaponAny lethal weapon sold and manufactured by Ammu-Nation, including replicas. Full list of weaponry included is listed in the weaponry database.
P.C.113Class 2 WeaponSingle Fire Pistols not manufactured by Ammu-Nation, including replicas. Full list of weaponry included is listed in the weaponry database.
P.C.114Class 3 WeaponAutomatic Pistols, Submachine Guns, Shotguns, including replicas. Full list of weaponry included is listed in the weaponry database.
P.C.115Class 4 WeaponAssault Rifles, Sniper Rifles, Muskets, including replicas. Full list of weaponry included is listed in the weaponry database.
P.C.116Class 5 WeaponIncendiary, and/or explosive devices, including replicas. Full list of weaponry included is listed in the weaponry database.
P.C.117PropertyAnything that is owned by a person or entity.
P.C.118Government PropertyAny property owned or issued by the Government.
P.C.119Reporter’s PrivilegeA “reporter” or “journalist” is protected under constitutional or statutory law, from being compelled to testify about confidential information or sources.
P.C.120RICORacketeering Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act.
P.C.121HUTHeld Until Trial, a procedure that only Judges use to stop dangerous people from re-entering society until trial.
P.C.122Speed Limits50 mph in a city and town, 100 mph on a divided highway/interstate
P.C.123General Intent CrimeOffenses where prosecution has to show a person committed a criminal act. No concern whether the accused intended to produce the specific result of that act.
P.C.124Specific Intent CrimeOffenses where prosecution has to show a person intended to commit an unlawful act, and specifically intended to violate the law.
P.C.125PlayStation PistolsVR weaponry are not under the weapon class system and are not illegal. It is advised you do not carry them on you in case they are mistaken for something else.
P.C.126NarcoticsPainkillers, Antibiotics, Fentanyl or OxyContin (Oxy), including synthetic alternatives.
P.C.127Lockpicks"Lockpicks" include Basic Lockpicks, Advanced Lockpicks, and Multitools.
P.C.128FirearmsAny weapon (including tasers and bean bag shot guns) which will or is designed to expel a projectile by the action of an explosion.
P.C.129Distilled SpiritsSpirits produced through the process of distillation.

Enhancements
Code Name Definition Multiplier Result
P.C.1101 Accessory Before The Fact (ACC-BTF) Any person not present when the crime itself is committed, but has knowledge of the crime before or after the fact, and may assist in its commission. 25% Seize
P.C.1102 Accessory After The Fact (ACC-ATF) Any person who aids or abets someone who has committed a crime after the crime has been committed with the intent to help the person avoid arrest or punishment. 75% Seize
P.C.1103 Aiding and Abetting (AAA) Any person who aids in the active commission of a crime shall be given the same punishment. 100% Seize
P.C.1104 Applicability (APP) Charges labelled with Per as defined in TITLE 1. can be stacked, taking care to only add additional times and fines if it states PER next to the time and fines. 100% Seize
P.C.1105 Attempt (ATT) A person who attempts to commit any crime but fails, is prevented, or is intercepted, shall be given the same punishment as if the offense was commited. 100% Seize
P.C.1106 Conspiracy (CON) A person who conspires to commit any crime shall be given the same punishment as if the offense was commited. 100% Seize
P.C.1107 Soliciting (SOL) A person who solicits for the commission or perpetration of any crime shall be given a similar punishments as if the offense was committed. 50% Seize
P.C.1108 Gang Enhancement (GE) Any criminal activity with 2 or more known gang members involved shall be given a harsher total time and fine to deter future offences. 150% -
P.C.1109 Protected Persons (PP) Any criminal activity targeted at a public servant or peace officer shall be given a harsher total time and fine to deter future offences. 140% -
P.C.1110 Public Menace (PM) Any person convicted of a serious felony who previously has been convicted of a serious felony is subject to an increase on their sentence per count of prior serious felony. (Manually add 600 months) 100% +600 Months Per
P.C.1111 Intent to Sell (ITS) A person who shows clear intent to sell; drugs, contraband or weaponry shall be given a harsher total time and fine to deter future offences. 150% -
P.C.1112 Protected Property (P-Prop) Any criminal activity targeted at a public servant's or peace officer's property or governement property shall be given a harsher total time and fine to deter future offences. 120% -
